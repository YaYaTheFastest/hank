Job cards

Engine Oil & Filter 200 hr
  1. Warm oil to operating temp, then shut down and remove the key.
  2. Drain crankcase fully.
  3. Replace filter M806419 — oil the gasket, hand-tighten ¾ turn past contact. confirm part vs manual
  4. Refill ~1.4 gal 15W-40 CK-4.
  5. Run, check for leaks, shut down, recheck level on the dipstick.
Reference: Operator's Manual — Engine Oil & Filter service. confirm page #
Hydraulic & Transmission Fluid 400 hr
  1. Park on level ground, lower all implements, relieve hydraulic pressure.
  2. Drain the case (12+ gal).
  3. Replace filter RE45864, clean the magnetic drain plug. confirm part vs manual
  4. Refill ~12 gal Hy-Gard J20D.
  5. Cycle the hydraulics, recheck level, check for leaks.
Reference: Operator's Manual — Transmission / Hydraulic service. confirm page #
Daily Pre-Use Walkaround Daily
  1. Check engine oil and coolant levels.
  2. Check hydraulic reservoir level.
  3. Look for leaks under the tractor and at the loader.
  4. Check tire condition and pressure.
  5. Test lights, horn, and that the seat-switch / safety start works.
Reference: Operator's Manual — Daily / pre-start checks. confirm page #
